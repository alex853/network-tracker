VATSIM Tracker for Google Earth

This service allows to observe VATSIM pilots in Google Earth.
It shows all connected pilots and their positions for last 30 minutes.
You may watch aircraft tracks, climbs and descends, time accelerations etc.

Open VATSIM Tracker for Google Earth

To use this service you need to install Google Earth on your PC.
You may download it here

Aircraft symbols
Flying
Pilot is online right now and is in air.
On ground
Pilot is online right now and is on ground.
Offline
Pilot is offline. In air and on ground pilots are shown by same symbol.


Track segment colours
blue segment Flying
Pilot is flying in air - starting and finishing points are in air
green segment On ground
Pilot is moving on ground - starting and finishing points are on ground.
orange segment Missing data
One or few snapshots are missing however pilot is online right now.
red segment Jump on ground
Pilot was on ground and moved his aircraft to another airport or parking.
gray segment Offline
Pilot is offline now. All segments are shown like this.
